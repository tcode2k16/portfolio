Vitalic

A health screening application that can calculate your heart rate based on a simple video.

  • Tech: Vue.js, Vuex, Element ui...
  • Time Spent: 24hr
  • Open Source: Yes

This is a project I built with a few friends during a 24hr hackathon. The app calculates a person's heart rate based on his blood flow. The app first locates a person's forehead and then magnifies the change of pixels in order to get the heart rate. From there, the app can get infomation about your blood pressure, mood, and much more.
